Game Features

Dark Dark Galaxy is a fast-paced action roguelike top-down shooter. Pilot a space drone to battle an invasive insectoid species. Illuminate the darkness and grow stronger as you travel to different star systems. Can you free our galaxy from the invaders?

  • Travel to dozens of unique star systems and slay all enemies to continue your journey across the galaxy
  • The arena layout changes after the first wave, adapt quickly and don't lose track of your surroundings
  • Use a wide variety of weapons and special abilities and rise to the challenge of mastering your kill efficiency 
  • Install chips to gain powerful traits
  • Unlock the full potential of your weapons by discovering powerful synergies
  • Customise the appearance of your space drone to match your style
  • Use cheats to fine-tune the difficulty and tailor the challenge to your liking
  • Earn XP to unlock colours, patterns, cheats, and drones
