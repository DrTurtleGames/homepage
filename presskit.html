Game Features

Dark Galaxy is an action based rogue-like top-down shooter. You pilot a space drone to oppose the insectoid like species called Minaripods. Illuminate the darkness and get stronger by travelling to different star systems. Can you free our galaxy from the invaders?

  • Travel to dozens of unique star systems and slay all enemies to continue the journey across the galaxy
  • The arena layout changes after the first wave - adapt and don't lose track of your surroundings
  • Use a variety of weapons and specials and rise to the challenge of mastering your kill efficiency
  • Install chips to gain special traits
  • Customise the appearance of your space drone
